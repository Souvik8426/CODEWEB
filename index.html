About

Techwiz was started in the year 2014 in the Computer Science and Engineering department. The motive of the club is to impart knowledge to students about emerging technologies like Big Data, RTOS, Quantum Computing, etc. Techwiz conducts hands-on workshops and seminars in various fields which cater and impart the technical knowledge to the young engineers in our campus. It provides the best platform to update and upgrade the recent advancements in the digital world. The club believes in knowledge sharing and encourages members to solve real-world problems by advanced methodologies.
